Admissions

Join our community of learners and achievers

Admission Process

1

Application

Submit a completed application form along with required documents. Forms are available at the school office or can be downloaded from our website.

2

Entrance Exam

All applicants are required to sit for an entrance examination to assess their academic level. Dates are communicated upon application.

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for an interview with the school administration.

4

Admission Letter

Successful candidates will receive an admission letter outlining the next steps, including fee payment and reporting date.

Admission Requirements

Form 1 (Grade 8) Entry

  • Completed primary school education (KCPE certificate)
  • Minimum KCPE score of 300 marks
  • Birth certificate
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Transfer letter from previous school (if applicable)
  • Medical report form

Other Forms (Transfers)

  • Report cards from previous school
  • Leaving certificate from previous school
  • Birth certificate
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Transfer letter
  • Medical report form

International Students

  • Equivalent academic records
  • Passport copies (student and parents)
  • Study permit
  • Two recent passport photos
  • Medical report form
  • Guardian's details in Kenya

Scholarships & Bursaries

Greenwood High School offers several scholarship opportunities to deserving students:

Academic Excellence Scholarship

For students with outstanding KCPE results (380 marks and above). Covers up to 100% of tuition fees.

Sports Scholarship

For students with exceptional talent in sports. Partial to full scholarships available.

Needy Students Fund

Bursaries for students from disadvantaged backgrounds with good academic performance.

Applications for scholarships should be submitted along with the admission application. Additional documents may be required depending on the scholarship type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Admission for Form 1 begins immediately after KCPE results are released. Admissions for other forms are ongoing throughout the year depending on availability of spaces.

Currently, we only admit boarding students to maintain our holistic education program. However, special arrangements can be made for students with medical conditions requiring day schooling.

We maintain a ratio of 1 teacher to 25 students to ensure personalized attention and quality learning.

We have partnered with trusted transport providers who offer safe and reliable transport services during opening and closing days at an additional cost.

Welcome to Greenwood High School

Greenwood High School is a premier educational institution in Kenya, committed to providing holistic education that combines academic excellence with moral and social development. Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals who will make positive contributions to society.

With state-of-the-art facilities, qualified teachers, and a conducive learning environment, we offer students the perfect platform to achieve their full potential.

Academic Departments

Explore our diverse academic departments and programs

Science Department

The Science Department at Greenwood High School is committed to fostering scientific inquiry and innovation. Our well-equipped laboratories and experienced teachers provide students with hands-on learning experiences.

Mathematics

Our mathematics program develops logical thinking and problem-solving skills through a comprehensive curriculum.

Head of Mathematics: Mr. David Ochieng

Physics

Students explore the fundamental principles of the physical world through theory and practical experiments.

Head of Physics: Mrs. Susan Mwende

Chemistry

Our chemistry program emphasizes laboratory skills and understanding chemical processes in everyday life.

Head of Chemistry: Mr. Paul Gitau

Biology

Students study living organisms and life processes, with opportunities for field studies and research.

Head of Biology: Mrs. Jane Wanjiru

Humanities Department

The Humanities Department offers subjects that explore human culture, society, and historical development, helping students understand the world around them.

History & Government

Students examine historical events and governmental systems to understand contemporary society.

Head of History: Mr. Samuel Njoroge

Geography

Our geography program includes physical and human geography with field study opportunities.

Head of Geography: Mrs. Grace Mumbi

CRE/IRE

Religious education that promotes moral values and understanding of different faiths.

Head of IRE: Mr. Mohamed Rashid

Languages Department

Our Languages Department focuses on developing communication skills in English, Kiswahili, and foreign languages, essential for academic and professional success.

English

We emphasize reading, writing, speaking and critical thinking skills in English.

Head of English: Mrs. Elizabeth Atieno

Kiswahili

Students develop proficiency in Kenya's national language and explore Swahili literature.

Head of Kiswahili: Mr. Ali Hassan

French

Our French program introduces students to the language and culture of French-speaking countries.

Head of French: Mrs. Marie Dupont

Technical Department

The Technical Department offers practical skills and knowledge in various technical fields, preparing students for careers in technology and applied sciences.

Computer Studies

Students learn programming, computer applications, and digital literacy skills.

Head of Computer Studies: Mr. Brian Otieno

Business Studies

Our business program covers accounting, commerce, and entrepreneurship skills.

Head of Business Studies: Mrs. Susan Akinyi

Agriculture

Practical agricultural skills combined with theoretical knowledge of food production.

Head of Agriculture: Mr. Peter Mwangi

Home Science

Students learn nutrition, clothing, and home management skills.

Head of Home Science: Mrs. Margaret Wangui

Co-Curricular Activities

At Greenwood High School, we believe in holistic education. Our co-curricular program includes:

Sports

Football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, swimming

Music & Drama

Choir, band, drama club, music festivals

Clubs & Societies

Debate, science club, journalism, wildlife club

Community Service

Environmental projects, charity work, mentorship

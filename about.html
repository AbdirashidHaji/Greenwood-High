About Greenwood High School

Learn about our history, mission, vision, and values

Our History

Founded in 1985, Greenwood High School began as a small community school with just 50 students and 5 teachers. Over the years, through dedication to quality education and community support, we have grown into one of the leading educational institutions in the region.

In 2005, we expanded our facilities to include modern science laboratories, a computer center, and a well-equipped library. Today, we serve over 800 students from diverse backgrounds, preparing them for success in Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide holistic, quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and social responsibility, preparing students to be productive members of society.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that produces well-rounded individuals equipped to meet the challenges of a dynamic world.
